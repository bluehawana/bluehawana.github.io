From the dust on the laptops, you can tell which one gets the most love.

The first one? My #PinebookPro. Last opened nearly half a year ago. The angry emojis? That’s from when I helped my daughter review math exam questions — let’s just say she wasn’t impressed. 🙃  
Truth is, without solid app support and ecosystem maturity, it’s been gathering dust more than doing real work. Now I've installed Kali on it — it's almost the best Linux OS for this device.

The second: a #LenovoThinkpadE15 Gen 3 running Windows 11. I open it maybe once a month — mostly to test a project on another platform or upload something to Google Drive. It does its job, but it’s not my go-to. 

And then there’s the third: my #MacminiM4Pro — 64GB RAM, 2TB SSD. Running 24/7 as my #homelab. No dust. No breaks. This is the one I actually live on.

So — what about you?  
Which machine at home do you actually *use most*, and why?
