Welcome to Hongzhi Li's professional website and LinkedIn Community Management services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you interact with our services, particularly our LinkedIn Community Management API integration.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data handling practices. This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and LinkedIn's Developer Program requirements.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 LinkedIn API Data

Through our LinkedIn Community Management API integration, we may collect:

  • LinkedIn profile information (name, headline, profile URL)
  • Public posts and activities you choose to share
  • Professional experience and skills data
  • Network connections (with explicit consent)
  • Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)

2.2 Website Data

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message)
  • Service booking information via Calendly integration
  • Website analytics (anonymized visitor data)
  • IP addresses and browser information (for security)

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device information and browser type
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Session data and cookies
  • API access logs and error reports

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Service Delivery: Provide LinkedIn community management services
  • Content Sync: Synchronize LinkedIn posts with website portfolio
  • Professional Services: Deliver consulting, development, and IT services
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and service requests

3.2 LinkedIn API Specific Uses

  • Display your LinkedIn content on our professional portfolio

  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 LinkedIn Integration

We integrate with LinkedIn's API under their Developer Program terms. LinkedIn data is processed according to:

  • LinkedIn's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • LinkedIn Developer Program Agreement
  • Your LinkedIn privacy settings and preferences

4.2 Service Providers

  • Calendly: Meeting scheduling (privacy policy: calendly.com/privacy)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without explicit consent.

5.1 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure API token management and rotation
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

5.2 Data Storage

  • Location: EU/US data centers with GDPR compliance
  • Retention: Data retained only as long as necessary
  • Deletion: Secure deletion upon request or service termination

5.3 Data Retention Periods

  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information

  • Restriction: Limit processing of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

6.2 LinkedIn API Control

  • Revoke API access through LinkedIn's privacy settings
  • Control visibility of LinkedIn content
  • Request removal of synchronized content

6.3 How to Exercise Rights

Contact us at hongzhili01@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days and may require identity verification.
