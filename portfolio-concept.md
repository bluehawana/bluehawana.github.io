# Senior Developer Portfolio Concept

## Design Direction
**Theme**: "Cross-Cultural Code Craftsman"
**Aesthetic**: Refined minimalism with subtle cultural accents
**Tone**: Sophisticated, professional, globally-minded

## Key Elements
1. Clean, elegant typography with subtle Eastern influences
2. Dark theme with accent colors representing your journey (Ürümqi → Dalian → Gothenburg)
3. Focused on technical storytelling rather than commercial promotion
4. Emphasis on your unique career transition story as a strength
5. Showcase open-source contributions and personal projects
6. Clear separation between technical expertise and business inquiries

## Structure
1. Hero Section - Journey-focused introduction
2. Technical Expertise - Skills matrix with proficiency indicators
3. Career Transition Story - Visual timeline of your unique path
4. Key Projects - Personal projects only (no commercial ventures)
5. Open Source Contributions - GitHub activity integration
6. Technical Writing - Blog posts and documentation samples
7. Professional Connect - LinkedIn, GitHub, contact (no business CTAs)
